Ethan Kerzner

I design and develop visualization software for expert users. For my graduate research, I work closely with specialists in various domains to understand their data analysis needs and propose visualization solutions to satisfy those needs.

I work with Miriar Meyer in the Vis Design Lab at the University of Utah School of Computing. This page has a select list of my projects. Check out my scholar profile for a full publication list.

Past Projects

Visual vulnerability analysis
