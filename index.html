Ethan Kerzner

I design and develop visualization software for expert users. For my graduate research, I work closely with specialists in various domains to understand their data analysis needs, explore designs to fulfill those needs, and realize the most promising designs in prototype software.

I am a PhD student working with Miriar Meyer in the Vis Design Lab at the University of Utah School of Computing.

This page has a select list of my projects. Check out my scholar profile for a full publication list.
