Ethan Kerzner

Computer scientist and visualization designer

I design and build visualization software that enables expert users to make sense of complex data. This is part of my research for a Ph.D. in computer science at the Vis Design Lab at the University of Utah. My dissertation focuses on user-centered design — it is about using creativity workshops to understand the analysis needs of expert users. It is based on my experience designing visualization software for neuroscientists, defense analysts, and physicians.

Before focusing on visualization I worked on various aspects of real-time graphics, including hardware, algorithms and applications. This included time at Nvidia, Intel, Activision-Blizzard, and the U.S. Army Research Laboratory.
